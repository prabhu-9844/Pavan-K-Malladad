About

Pavankumar K Malladada

Pavankumar K Malladada is a self-employed individual with a fervent dedication to community development and political activism, serving as a prominent member of the BJP Yuva Morcha (BJYM) Karnataka. Having pursued their studies at Karnataka University, Dharwad, India since 2017, they have become deeply ingrained in the fabric of Ranibennur, Karnataka, both as a resident and as an active participant in local initiatives. Their single status underscores their commitment to their professional and political engagements, where they channel their energy into advocating for youth empowerment and socio-economic progress. With a strong sense of purpose and a vision for a more inclusive society, Pavankumar K Malladada strives to make a lasting impact on their community, inspiring others to join them in shaping a brighter future for Ranibennur and beyond.
